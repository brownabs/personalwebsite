About Me

Hello! I'm Abbey Brown.

Last year I graduated from Nashville Software School's six month bootcamp with a certification for Full Stack Web Development. Soon after, I accepted a position as a tech analyst where I immensely loved the job and company. Due to the pandemic, my contract ended, but another door opened with a teaching position for NSS. The growth I experienced by teaching adult learners software development was immense. I was able to learn the advantageous skill of analyzing and understanding other's code, while broadening my own abilities. I'm eager to begin my career as a software developer, and continue to contribute to the Nashville technology community.
